About Me

Hi! I'm Tejender Kaur, a freelance photographer based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. I'm a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing genuine moments and turning them into timeless memories. Whether it's a wedding, family session, or branding shoot, I believe every story is unique—and my goal is to tell yours through authentic, beautifully crafted images.

With a relaxed approach and a keen eye for detail, I strive to make every session comfortable, fun, and unforgettable. I specialize in landscape, portrait, and street photography. My goal is to capture the world in its most authentic form.
